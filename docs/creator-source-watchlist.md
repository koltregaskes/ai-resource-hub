# Creator Source Watchlist

Reviewed: 20 Apr 2026

## Purpose

This is a short internal watchlist for creator-led material that is useful to the AI Resource Hub.

The goal is not to turn the site into a transcript archive.

The goal is to identify creators whose videos regularly contain:

- durable workflow ideas
- practical model-comparison signal
- operational insights that do not show up in formal benchmark tables
- useful educational material for readers who want plain-English explanations

## Use Rules

- link to the original source
- summarise the concept in our own words
- quote sparingly
- do not republish full transcripts
- separate opinion from measured evidence
- label anecdotal workflow advice clearly

## Recommended Output Shapes

Theo material is best turned into:

- glossary entries
- short educational blog posts
- methodology sidebars
- reading lists
- model or tooling caveat callouts

It is not best turned into:

- raw transcript dumps
- long quote pages
- unsupported benchmark claims

## First Build Candidates

The easiest useful pieces to create from this lane are:

- a short educational page on verification harnesses
- a short explainer on why models can feel worse without the weights changing
