# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This repository is operated as a focused, factual AI reference project.

Contributors, reporters, and reviewers are expected to:

- be respectful and specific
- focus on evidence, sources, and reproducible claims
- avoid hostility, harassment, or personal attacks
- avoid spam, promotion, or low-signal argument threads
- keep contributions tightly scoped to the repository's stated policy

## Unacceptable behaviour

The following are not acceptable here:

- harassment, abuse, or intimidation
- discriminatory or hateful language
- deliberate misinformation
- bad-faith pile-ons or antagonistic behaviour
- off-topic campaigning, marketing, or repetitive solicitation
- sharing private or sensitive information without permission

## Maintainer responsibilities

The maintainer may edit, close, limit, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, and discussions that are inconsistent with this policy or the repository's contribution policy.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies across repository issues, pull requests, reviews, comments, and related public collaboration spaces attached to this project.

## Reporting

If you experience or observe unacceptable behaviour, contact the maintainer privately at:

- `kolin@tregaskes.co.uk`

Maintainer decisions on repository moderation are final.
